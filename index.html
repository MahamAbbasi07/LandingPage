FEATURES

Deal Now is a two-sided communication and marketplace platform through which consumers can communicate/search with the food service providers. Consumer can easily search the food deals, confirm order and process payment, receive promotions & deals, receive any update or new offering

  • Geo location

    This app auto pushes deals to users in the area based on their interest profile.

  • Deals on nearby stores

  • Share list with your Friends and family

    Communicate with store owner in chatbox for additional details and queries.

  • Shopping list

    Customize your shopping list before you order.

  • Trending deals

    Shop the most Popular and Trending deals. Save money on these hot selling products.

AI FEATURES

With the help of an AI Assistant, you can search for household and grocery items on discounts and promotion within your vicinity.

  • Create customize shopping list by speaking to a virtual assitant on your smartphone.
  • AI assistant will keep track of search history and based on your preferences display discounted household & grocery items.
  • Maintains a database of your preferences both in the searched and purchased items as well as favourite brands.
  • Analyse shopping behavior & shows detailed analytics.
  • Suggest deals on frequent purchased or favourite items through optional push notification.
  • Shows "The Trending Deals" in your favourite stores.
  • Compare prices of any item and make recommendations.

AI & BENEFITS TO CPG

The database is turned into an actionable insights for coporate industry through usage of intelligent algorithms.

  • AI assistant help retailers to deal with customer queries related any product, its availability or facilities, responded by an assistant with details of the required item & in-store location of the product.
  • Insights into buyer personnas and consumer spending habits help retailers to increase sales by creating best action patterns for promotions.
  • Actionable insights bring tremendous benefits to CPG such as enabling predictive merchandising, programmatic advertising, market forecasting, in-store visual monitoring & surveillance, and location-based marketing.
